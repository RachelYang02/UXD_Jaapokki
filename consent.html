CONSENT NARRATIVE

a form we gave out to our users before any user visit to ensure that we were all on the same page.


Hi, my name is (Gaby, Sean, Jordan, Rachel), and I’m a student at Olin College of Engineering. I’m taking a user experience design course and my team is designing an app to connect people who want to donate specific goods to nonprofits and other groups that need those goods via a software interface. We were wondering if you would be willing to take some time to talk to us and give us feedback regarding any specific features of our application design. We appreciate your time no matter the amount. We will not be able to compensate you for your time, but we hope that you will enjoy the experience.

We can discuss the specifics of the meeting further and how the experience will go, but we would likely be interviewing or observing you and taking notes about your interaction with our design. We’ll ask permission before taking video or audio recordings or pictures, and we would keep any private information confidential and change your name for our notes/write-ups. Of you course, you can revoke consent for any of this at any time.

Thank you so much, and we look forward to hearing back from you!
Team Jaapokki (Gaby, Jordan, Rachel, and Sean)




Information collection techniques that will be used

In order to accurately reflect the nature and contents of our interactions, we will be collecting information during interviews. We will ask before photographing, filming, or recording any part of our interactions. We do plan to take notes on the interactions, but they will be pseudonymised and private information will not be shared.




Request for approval to use the techniques identified

During our interaction(s), are you comfortable with us photographing you? filming you? recording our conversation? You may, of course, revoke consent given for any of these techniques at any time.





User Experience Design 2017

Gaby Clarke, Sean Foley, Jordan Van Duyne, and Rachel Yang

Special thanks to the brilliant teaching team: Amon Millner and Marco Morales.
